About

Palm surfboards is a custom surfboard business showroom and workshop based out of Taranaki, New Zealand. Meet us in person at our showroom, viewings available by appointment come in and say hi. Our surfboards are locally made and are of highest quality, refined and finished by craftsmen Hamish Mckie built to last. Palm surfboards draw inspiration from a wide range of surfboard designs and wave riding expressions. Deeply influenced by the rich culture of our surfing history. Surfing is a constant source of inspiration for us in all types of conditions and all kinds of boards. Our vision is to make custom boards for all types of waves and people.
